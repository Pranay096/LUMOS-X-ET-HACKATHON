### Section 41 — Protective Equipment in Hazardous Processes
Every worker in a manufacturing process classified as hazardous must be provided with, and required to use, suitable protective equipment as specified by the occupier's safety classification. Failure to enforce protective equipment usage in a designated hazardous zone constitutes a compliance violation reportable to the Chief Inspector of Factories.
*(Internally mapped to: APM-SOP-014, Zone B PPE Requirements)*

### Section 38 — Precautions Against Dangerous Fumes, Gases, and Energy Sources
No worker shall be required or permitted to enter a confined space, or work on equipment carrying residual electrical, hydraulic, or pneumatic energy, unless the space or equipment has been certified safe for entry through an approved isolation and verification procedure.
*(Internally mapped to: APM-SOP-017, Lockout-Tagout Procedure)*

### Section 45 — Maintenance of Safe Working Conditions for Equipment Above Ground Level
Work performed at elevation shall require fall-arrest equipment conforming to applicable standards, anchored to a verified anchor point, for all work above the threshold height specified in the facility's internal safety classification.
*(Internally mapped to: APM-SOP-014 Section 6, APM-SOP-021 Working at Height)*

### Section 52 — Maintenance Records and Audit Trail
The occupier shall maintain auditable records of all preventive and corrective maintenance performed on equipment classified as High or Critical criticality, including the procedure revision in effect at the time of service, for a minimum retention period as specified by the regulatory authority.
*(Internally mapped to: APM-EML-002 Criticality Classification; all APM-SOP maintenance procedures)*

### Section 59 — Reporting of Near-Miss and Incident Events
All incidents resulting in equipment damage, personnel injury, or near-miss events with potential for injury shall be documented and reported to the EHS Manager within 24 hours, with root cause analysis completed within 7 working days for High or Critical criticality equipment.
*(Internally mapped to: Incident Reporting Register, APM-INC series)*
