# INCIDENT / WORK ORDER REPORT

**Report No:** APM-INC-052
**Equipment ID:** CMP-301 (Air Compressor Unit 1)
**Date of Incident:** 2025-09-12
**Reported By:** P. Verma, Maintenance Technician
**Work Order No:** APM-WO-2025-0855
**Severity:** Low (no downtime, scheduled maintenance finding)

---

### Description
During scheduled preventive maintenance, technician observed elevated discharge air temperature (above normal operating range by approximately 8°C) on CMP-301. No immediate failure; flagged for monitoring.

### Conditions at Time of Inspection
Routine 90-day PM check. Ambient conditions normal. Air filter found moderately clogged — likely contributor to elevated discharge temperature.

### Action Taken
Air filter replaced. Discharge temperature returned to normal range after filter replacement and 15-minute run test.

### Root Cause
Clogged intake air filter restricting airflow, causing elevated compression temperature.

### Recommendation
No SOP change required. Continue standard 90-day filter replacement interval. Technician noted filter clogging appeared faster than expected for this interval — recommend monitoring over next 2 cycles before considering interval change.

### Status
Closed.
