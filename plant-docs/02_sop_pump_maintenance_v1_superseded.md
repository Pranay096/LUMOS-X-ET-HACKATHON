### 1. Purpose
To define the preventive maintenance procedure for the Hydraulic Coolant Pump (PMP-204) to ensure continued reliable operation and coolant supply to Zone B machining lines.

### 2. Scope
Applies to all maintenance personnel performing scheduled or unscheduled maintenance on PMP-204.

### 3. Maintenance Interval
Preventive maintenance shall be performed every **180 days**.

### 4. Procedure
1. Isolate electrical supply to PMP-204 at the local panel.
2. Close inlet and outlet isolation valves.
3. Drain residual coolant into designated collection drum.
4. Inspect mechanical seal for visible wear or leakage.
5. Inspect bearing housing for excessive play.
6. Check coupling alignment using straight-edge method.
7. Top up bearing lubricant (Grade: Servo Mesa 32 or equivalent).
8. Reassemble, open isolation valves, restore power.
9. Run pump for 10 minutes and check for abnormal vibration or noise.
10. Log completion in the Maintenance Register.

### 5. PPE Requirements
Safety gloves and safety goggles.

### 6. Known Issues
None documented at time of issue.
