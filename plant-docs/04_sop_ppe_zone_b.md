# ASHOKA PRECISION MANUFACTURING PVT. LTD.
## Standard Operating Procedure: Personal Protective Equipment Requirements — Zone B (Machining Bay)

**Document No:** APM-SOP-014
**Revision:** 4 (CURRENT)
**Effective Date:** 2025-11-01
**Prepared By:** A. Krishnan, EHS Manager
**Approved By:** V. Nair, Plant Manager

> **STATUS: CURRENT.**

---

### 1. Purpose
To define mandatory personal protective equipment (PPE) for all personnel entering Zone B (Machining Bay), which houses high-pressure hydraulic equipment, rotating machinery, and robotic welding operations.

### 2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors entering Zone B, without exception.

### 3. Zone Classification
Zone B is classified as a **Restricted PPE Zone** under the Plant Safety Classification Register. Entry without complete PPE is a Category 2 safety violation under Section 41 of the Factory Act provisions referenced in APM-REG-001.

### 4. Mandatory PPE — All Personnel
1. Safety helmet (Class B, non-conductive)
2. Safety goggles or full face shield (face shield mandatory within 2 metres of WLD-077 robotic welding cell during active welding cycles)
3. Steel-toe safety boots
4. High-visibility vest
5. Cut-resistant gloves (Level 3 minimum) when handling sheet metal or near PRS-088

### 5. Additional PPE — Task-Specific
1. **Hydraulic press operation (PRS-088):** Full face shield, hearing protection (>85 dB zone), no loose clothing or jewelry.
2. **Welding operations (WLD-077):** Welding-grade face shield or auto-darkening helmet, flame-resistant apron, welding gloves — mandatory even for personnel only observing within the 2-metre exclusion radius.
3. **Pump maintenance (PMP-204):** Face shield in addition to standard PPE (added under APM-SOP-009 Rev. 2 following seal-related splash incidents).

### 6. Harness Requirement
Any work performed above 1.8 metres elevation within Zone B (e.g., overhead crane maintenance, conveyor inspection platforms) requires a full-body harness with dual lanyard, anchored per APM-SOP-021 (Working at Height).

### 7. Non-Compliance
Entry into Zone B without mandatory PPE shall result in immediate work stoppage and escalation to the EHS Manager. Repeated violations are subject to disciplinary action per HR Policy 7.3.
