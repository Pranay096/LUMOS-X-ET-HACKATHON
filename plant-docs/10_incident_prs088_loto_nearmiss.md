# INCIDENT / WORK ORDER REPORT

**Report No:** APM-INC-061
**Equipment ID:** PRS-088 (Hydraulic Press Station)
**Date of Incident:** 2025-10-03
**Reported By:** A. Krishnan, EHS Manager
**Work Order No:** N/A (Near-Miss — no work order, safety observation)
**Severity:** High (near-miss, no injury — Category 1 LOTO violation)

---

### Description
During a routine EHS walkthrough, a contract technician was observed beginning mechanical adjustment work on PRS-088 without completing lockout-tagout per APM-SOP-017. Hydraulic pressure had not been verified at zero. Technician was immediately stopped before any energy release occurred.

### Conditions at Time of Incident
Technician was new to site (third day on assignment) and had received PPE briefing but had not yet completed full LOTO training sign-off. Permit-to-Work had been issued but LOTO steps 4–6 (isolation, lock/tag, zero-energy verification) had been skipped.

### Action Taken
Work stopped immediately. Technician escorted off the floor pending retraining. Contractor's supervising agency notified. Full LOTO completed by a certified plant technician before work resumed.

### Root Cause
Inadequate verification of contractor LOTO certification status before permit issuance. Permit approval process did not cross-check training completion status.

### Recommendation
1. Add mandatory LOTO certification check as a gating step before Permit-to-Work approval for all contractors.
2. Treat this as a Category 1 reportable event per APM-REG-001 Section 38, despite no energy release having occurred, due to the severity of potential consequence.

### Status
Closed. Permit approval process updated. Reported to Plant Manager and logged per APM-REG-001 Section 59 (near-miss reporting, completed within 24 hours).
